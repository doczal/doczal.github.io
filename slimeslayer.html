Slime Slayer Iro

Slime Slayer Iro is a flash game that I worked on during my free time back in 2011. The game features a little wizard who must fend off colorful slimes by firing spells at them. The player must try to constantly hit 3 same-colored slimes in a row to quickly get their mana back. I unfortunately never got around to finishing the game.

Tools Used
ActionScript 3, FlashDevelop, Flixel Library
