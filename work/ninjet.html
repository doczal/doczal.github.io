--- title: Ninjet description: A shoot 'em up game developed for Android devices. layout: project-info type: Game Development permalink: /ninjet/ ---

Ninjet is an endless top-down shoot 'em up game made for Android devices.

I worked in a small team of four. I was the programmer as well as a contributing artist.

The game was developed using the Stencyl engine, and was released on the Google Play Store on March 7, 2016.
