South West Allied Therapies is a team of health specialists based in Western Australia. They provide services such as speech and occupational therapy. They are mobile, and often travel to deliver their service to clients.

They had just set up their business at the time, so they required both a company logo as well as a website.

I decided to use Wordpress to build their site, mainly to force myself to learn Wordpress. I started out with a basic Bootstrap theme, and customized the site from there.
