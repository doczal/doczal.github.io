Ninjet

Ninjet is a mobile shoot 'em up game currently still in development. It features endless gameplay with a variety of enemy wave types and bosses, as well as allowing the player to switch between a jet and robot form on the fly. I am working in a small team of four, where I am both the programmer and a contributing artist.
